Our Story

Cooked with faith,
served with love

Jain Rasoi was born in 2019 from a simple belief: Jain families in Vasundhara deserve food they can trust completely — not just "veg" food, but truly Sattvic, Jain-principles-compliant meals prepared with devotion.

Our founder, Pratibha Ben, learned these recipes from her mother in Surat. Every morning by 6 AM, our kitchen fills with the aroma of fresh-ground spices, clean dal, and hand-rolled rotis — prepared without a single root vegetable touching the countertop.

We serve working professionals, students, and families who value their faith and their health equally. No compromise, ever.

"अहिंसा परमो धर्मः — Non-violence is the highest dharma. Our food reflects this in every bite."

— Pratibha Ben, Founder, Jain Rasoi
